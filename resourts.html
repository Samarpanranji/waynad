WILD PLANET BANA HEIGHTS


Situated in Wayanad, 5.5 km from Banasura Hill, WILD PLANET BANA HEIGHTS features accommodation with a garden, free private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. The property is around 10 km from Meenmutty Falls, 10 km from Banasura Sagar Dam and 15 km from Karlad Lake. The resort has family rooms.

Speaking English, Hindi, Malayalam and Tamil at the 24-hour front desk, staff are ready to help around the clock.

Jeevess Ayurveda Resort


Located in Wayanad, 8.5 km from Pookode Lake, Jeevess Ayurveda Resort provides accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness centre and a garden. Featuring a concierge service, this property also provides guests with a bowling alley. The accommodation features room service, a 24-hour front desk and currency exchange for guests.
At the resort, every room is equipped with a desk, bed linen and a balcony with a garden view. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Jeevess Ayurveda Resort also provide guests with free WiFi, while selected rooms have a mountain view. All units at the accommodation include air conditioning and a safety deposit box.
A continental, Asian or vegetarian breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. At Jeevess Ayurveda Resort you will find a restaurant serving Indian, local and Asian cuisine. Vegetarian, halal and gluten-free options can also be requested.
The resort offers a children's playground. You can play darts at Jeevess Ayurveda Resort, and the area is popular for hiking and fishing.
